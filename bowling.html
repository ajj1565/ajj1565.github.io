Stonewall Chicago - Bowling

Bowling is a fun way to get to know eachother in a more intimate indoor setting

Logistics

  • Who: You! (And 5 other people - it's teams of 6)
  • What: Bowling? Duh?
  • When: There are 2 seasons: One in the summer and one in the winter
  • Where: Waveland Bowl (3700 N Western Ave, Chicago, IL 60618)
  • Why: Because it's super fun and you can get to know a new group of people each time you play!
  • How: Use the registration link below to go to our LeagueApps site and register!

Season Timing and Registration

Seasons for Bowling take place in the Summer and Winter each year.

  • Summer Season 2019: Tuesdays, June 11th - August 13th
  • Winter Season 2019: Mondays, November 11th - January 27th
    • No Games 12/23 and 12/30 for Christmas and New Years

Registration usually opens 5 weeks ahead of the season, so please keep an eye out for more details
