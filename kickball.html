Stonewall Chicago - Kickball

Have a great time with the largest LGBT+ Kickball League in Chicago!

Logistics

  • Who: Teams of 20 people
  • What: Kicking the balls, rounding the bases, you know the drill
  • When: 2 Seasons: Spring and Fall
  • Where: Waveland Fields (3650 N Recreation Dr, Chicago, IL 60613)
  • Why: It's a great way to spend a sunny Sunday afternoon while making new friends
  • How: Use the registration link below to go to our LeagueApps site and register!

Season Timing and Registration

Seasons for Kickball take place in the Spring and Fall
